Trump Used Twitter...AGAIN

Meryl Streep can add one more to her stash of honors, being the subject of a Donald Trump Twitter rant.

The actress’ lifetime achievement award speech criticizing the President-elect Sunday night earned retaliation from Trump Monday morning.

“Meryl Streep, one of the most over-rated actresses in Hollywood, doesn't know me but attacked last night at the Golden Globes,” he posted just after 6 a.m., inserting a hyphen into "overrated."
